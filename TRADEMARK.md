## FAQ

**Q: Can I use "WireDSL" in my project name?**  
A: Only if it's clear it's a derived project. Use "based-on-wiredsl" or "wiredsl-plugins" style naming.

**Q: Can I fork WireDSL and call it something else?**  
A: Yes! You're free to fork under MIT and rename to anything. Just follow attribution guidelines.

**Q: Can I use WireDSL in my company name?**  
A: No. Contact us if you have a specific business need.

**Q: Is the source code open-source but the name proprietary?**  
A: Yes. MIT covers code, but trademark is separate intellectual property.

**Q: Can I create a "WireDSL competitor" product?**  
A: Yes, but you must use a different name. You're free to fork the code under MIT.

**Q: What about community extensions and plugins?**  
A: Community extensions are encouraged! Use naming like:

- "wiredsl-plugin-charts"
- "wiredsl-export-figma"
- "my-tool-with-wiredsl"
